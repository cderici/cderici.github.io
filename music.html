Listening

I have oddly diverse range of musical taste for listening. I'm a huge fan of progressive and heavy metal. My favorite bands include Dream Theater, Pain of Salvation, Ayreon, Judas Priest, Savatage, the usual classics.

However nowadays I also listen to modern jazz, e.g. E.S.T., some classical ballads like Julie London, Barbra Streisand, Ella Fitzgerald etc., or some straight up classical music like Vivaldi, or Tchaikovsky.

Also every now and then I come across with some contemporary projects such as Submotion Orchestra.

Playing

Playing is a whole another thing for sure, than listening. People usually want to play what they enjoy listening. I like to play progressive rock/metal and hard rock with the guitar.

I played some piano a while ago, I'd like to play some more, but don't have much time to put effort in practicing it. But still, my main instrument is still the electric guitar.
