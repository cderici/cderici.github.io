Hall of Fame

I dedicated this page to honor the grand-masters of our art. It is out of my individual respect, hall of fame, containing our old grand-masters. Obviously, it's impossible to list all of them here, so I'll just list an unordered subset. However, I'm sure the rest of them will excuse my lack of experience.

If you see that I missed some other great logician/computer scientist that should be placed here, please send an email to me, so we'll honor him/her as well.
